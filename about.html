About Us

Let's know about our startup studio model

At DCI, high-Impact Startup Studio, we integrate impact-driven funding, co-building ventures, and ecosystem collaboration to develop sustainable businesses in sharing economy, green economy and circular economy.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

The pioneering High Impact Startup Studio where investments and businesses create deep, meaningful impact while ensuring financial sustainability.

Mission

Co-build and co-invest businesses that redefine sharing, green and circular economy, fostering harmony between people, profit, and the planet.

Values

Integrity, innovation, empathy.

Our Approach

  • We co-build with founders from idea to product-market fit.
  • We focus on sustainable growth and strong unit economics.
  • We provide operational resources across product, engineering, and go-to-market.

Our Verticals

Three core investment verticals that drive our impact:

Sharing Economy

Unlocking value through asset-sharing models (cars, homes, skills).

Green Economy

Low-carbon, clean, and efficient production for sustainability.

Circular Economy

Minimizing waste by extending resource use and reuse cycles.

Our Uniqueness

What makes DCI unique?
  • Co-Founders rooted in conscious leadership.
  • Ecosystem-first approach.
  • Deep co-creation with every founder.

Leadership

Our leadership team brings deep experience from startups, venture capital, and product engineering. We believe in mentorship, transparency, and long-term partnership.
