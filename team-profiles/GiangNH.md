Ms. Giang is a Founder and CEO of MDJ, owns the FaFiFun brand, a startup of DCI studio. FaFiFun focuses on providing financial training programs for Vietnamese children.
With the mission of equipping the young generation with financial thinking, technological thinking, life skills and entrepreneurial spirit, FaFiFun designs high-quality programs with diverse forms - from classes, materials, learning tools, to practical experience activities.

### Overview
Giang is an entrepreneur and speaker in financial education, activating intelligence in over 1,000 Vietnamese children.
- Pioneer in early financial education for kids in Vietnam.
- 11+ years in finance, startups, investment; guided thousands of students via practical programs.
- Author: Mom, Where Did the Money Go?; co-author of 4 textbooks.
- TikTok: +130k followers.
- Values: Instill personality, responsibility, kindness through financial education.
