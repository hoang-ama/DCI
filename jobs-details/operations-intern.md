# Operations Intern

### Company & Location
- Company: Chungxe (Vehicle Rental and Sharing Platform)
- Department: Business / Operations
- Location: Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
- Employment Type: Internship (Full-time commitment preferred)

### Role Summary
This is a hands-on role perfect for a student or recent graduate eager to gain experience in the shared mobility space. You will support daily operations, ensuring smooth vehicle turnover and high customer satisfaction.

### Key Responsibilities
* Assist the Operations Manager with inventory tracking and documentation of vehicle fleet (cars and motorbikes).
* Coordinate vehicle maintenance, cleaning, and preparation for new rentals.
* Process customer inquiries and resolve minor service issues efficiently.
* Contribute to optimizing operations workflows to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

### Requirements
* Currently enrolled in or recently graduated with a degree in Business Administration, Logistics, or a related field.
* Excellent communication (Vietnamese and basic English) and problem-solving skills.
* Highly organized, proactive, and meticulous attention to detail.
* Familiarity with Ho Chi Minh City logistics is a strong plus.

### The Opportunity
Gain practical experience at Vietnam's leading vehicle sharing platform, with potential for a full-time position upon successful completion of the internship.
