# Digital Marketing Specialist

### Company & Location
- Company: DCI Studio (Central Team)
- Department: Marketing
- Location: Hanoi, Vietnam
- Employment Type: Full-time

### Role Summary
The DCI Studio marketing team supports the growth of all portfolio companies. You will be a hands-on digital expert, driving performance and brand visibility for 3-5 high-potential ventures simultaneously.

### Key Responsibilities
* Develop and execute paid advertising campaigns across Google, Facebook, and local Vietnamese platforms.
* Manage SEO strategy and content optimization to improve organic traffic and domain authority for multiple brand websites.
* Track, analyze, and report on campaign performance, providing actionable insights to C-level founders.
* Collaborate with in-house content and design teams to produce high-converting assets.

### Requirements
* 2+ years of experience managing significant performance marketing budgets.
* Proficiency in Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, and Google Analytics.
* Strong analytical skills with an ability to translate data into strategic marketing plans.
* Experience in the E-commerce, Tech, or Mobility sectors is highly valued.

### Grow With Us
Work directly with the Founding Partners and gain exposure to diverse, high-impact business models in the DCI ecosystem.
