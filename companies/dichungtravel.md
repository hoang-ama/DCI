### Overview
DiChung Travel offers personalized and group travel services, focusing on sustainable tourism and unique cultural experiences. 
It develops experiential travel products at affordable prices through optimal operations and resource sharing during travel
### Vision
Leading convenient and authentic travel experiences provider. 
### Mision
Popularizing sustainable experiential tourism products through optimal activities throughout the trip.
### Service & Product 
- Experience tour (with tour guide): Co-travel products are designed for different groups: families, friends, singles, children... designed or led by travel experts.
- Self-guided tour: includes air combo and land combo. Basic packages include transportation and accommodation 
- Retail utilities: support the process before, during and after travel (visa, tourguide, activities, F&B, souvenir.)
### Customer segment 
B2C, B2B
