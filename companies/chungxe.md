### Overview
Chungxe is the Vietnam's leading online platform for self-drive vehicle rental services. Its platform offers a wide range of vehicle ranging from basic motorbikes to luxury cars on rent for hourly, daily, and monthly basis that enable users to book vehicles using their mobile phones and travel as per their plans in a hassle-free manner.
### Vision
Become a vehicle sharing platform leader in Southeast Asia
### Mission
Vehicle sharing in Vietnam can become an alternative to vehicle ownership and traditional vehicle rental, offering several benefits:
- Increased accessibility: More people can afford to own a car.
- Optimized vehicle usage: Vehicles are used more efficiently, reducing the number of cars on the road.
- Cost savings: Both individuals and countries can save money on vehicle ownership and rental costs.
- Reduced traffic congestion: Fewer cars on the road lead to less traffic.
- Lower pollution levels: Fewer cars mean less pollution, contributing to a cleaner environment.
