### Overview
AgriX is a pioneer in the field of high-tech applications (Drones, AI, Big Data, IoT...) in agricultural development in Vietnam, managing a huge ecosystem including 500,000 hectares of rice and crop areas, more than 4,000 agricultural Drone pilots and an agricultural Drone distribution, maintenance and training system nationwide.
### Vision
Becoming a pioneer and leader in Digital Agriculture (AgriTech) in the Southeast region.
### Mision
Developing high-tech agriculture in a sustainable way: 
- Applying high, environmentally friendly technology. 
- Increasing productivity and quality of agricultural products. 
- Increasing competitiveness, export value, improving farmers' lives.
### Signature product 
Providing agricultural services using Drones such as: surveying and mapping, fertilizing, pesticide spraying, rice sowing
### Distribution product
- Agricultural Drones from DJI and XAG brands 
- Drone warranty and repair service center 
- Drone training academy 
### Customer segment 
B2C, B2B
