### Overview
Dichung Transport is a transportation company that focuses on exploiting wasted means of transportation including transporting people and transporting goods. It combines asset management methods, leasing or investing in assets with strategic value.
### Vision
Leading shared transportation company.
### Mision
Provide transportation service that more affordable, accessible and more environmental friendly.
### Service & Product 
- Transporting people (renting a car with a driver, renting a self-driving car, hiring a driver) 
- Transporting goods 
- Other value-added services (providing drivers, supporting procedures for participating in transportation business, advertising on vehicles, vehicle maintenance, consumable products/services for drivers, finance, insurance...)
### Customer segment 
B2C, B2B
