Privacy Policy

This policy explains how DCI collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website.

DCI Privacy Policy

Effective (last updated): Nov 13, 2025

1. Principles & Applicable Law

We process personal data in compliance with Vietnam’s Personal Data Protection Decree (PDPD, Decree 13/2023) and other relevant laws and guidance. We also follow applicable international best practices and guidance where relevant.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect (as applicable):

  • Account information;
  • Technical data such as device identifiers, IP addresses, logs, cookies, SDKs and pixels;
  • Support and communication records.

3. Legal Bases & Purposes

Purposes: We process personal data to operate and maintain services to verify identity and prevent fraud, to provide customer/partner support and handle complaints, and to improve our services, analytics and product development (primarily using anonymized or aggregated data; personal data is used only when a valid legal basis exists). We may also process data for marketing, advertising and measurement where we obtain consent and provide opt-out options when required. We process data when necessary for legal compliance and to protect our legal rights.

Legal bases: Consent is a general legal basis where required. Where applicable, we may rely on statutory grounds and other limited exceptions set out in the PDPD (for example, legal obligations, vital interests, or performance of a contract).

4. Sharing / Processors

We share or entrust data on a need-to-know basis with third parties under data processing agreements that impose purpose limitation, security controls, sub-processor rules and audit rights. Typical categories include:

  • IT, hosting and cloud providers;
  • Marketing and channel partners (referrals, joint campaigns, attribution, fraud prevention and coordinated support);
  • Legal disclosures to comply with law or protect rights.

5. Cookies / SDKs / Pixels

We use cookies, SDKs and pixels for convenience, anti-fraud, analytics, and ad measurement. Disabling these technologies in your browser or device may limit functionality or features.

6. Cross-Border Transfers

Personal data may be processed or stored outside Vietnam. We comply with PDPD requirements for cross-border transfers, including conducting transfer impact assessments and submitting notifications/updates to the Ministry or using consent and contractual safeguards where required.

7. Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law, after which data is deleted or anonymized securely.

8. Data Subject Rights

You may exercise rights to be informed, to access, to rectification, to deletion, to restriction (requests implemented by us within 72 hours where feasible), to data portability, to object, to withdraw consent, and to complain or pursue remedies (including complaints, denunciation, litigation or claims for damages), in accordance with applicable law.

9. Children’s Data

For children aged 7 or older, we require both the child’s and their guardian’s consent for processing personal data.

10. Security

We implement organizational, technical and physical safeguards to protect personal data, including access governance, encryption, audit logging, vulnerability management and incident response.

11. DPO / Contact

We appoint a Data Protection Officer (or maintain a responsible department) to manage data protection matters. For rights requests or other privacy enquiries, contact: contact@dcinvest.vn (attn: DPO).

12. Changes & Language

We may update this policy from time to time; changes take effect when posted on this page. In case of discrepancies among translations, the Vietnamese version prevails. Nothing in this policy prejudices mandatory Vietnamese consumer protections.
