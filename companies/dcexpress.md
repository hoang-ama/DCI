### Overview
DC Express provides fast and reliable express delivery services for both B2C and B2B clients, ensuring timely and secure package transportation in Logistics.
### Vision
Not stopping at providing just a transportation technology solution, Dichung Express also aspires to accelerate the transition of Vietnam's economy to sustainable, environmentally friendly development. “Shared thinking” brings people closer together – Working together for a better life in Vietnam with a common vision of a better future generation.
### Mision
DC Express believes that every Vietnamese person has the right to enjoy a better quality of life. Therefore, our transportation technology platform always aims to: 
- Help transportation units optimize resources and minimize loss due to idle assets. 
- Help each person access higher quality services at lower costs. 
- Reduce pressure on transport infrastructure & reduce environmental pollution. 
- Create a wise consumer community.
### Signature product 
Pick up and return goods at the door with same day delivery
