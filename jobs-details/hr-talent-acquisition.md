# HR & Talent Acquisition

### Company & Location
- Company: DCI Studio (Central Team)
- Department: HR & Administration
- Location: Hanoi, Vietnam
- Employment Type: Full-time

### Role Summary
You will play a crucial role in building the talent pipeline for DCI's high-impact ventures. This position focuses heavily on sourcing and acquiring highly skilled individuals, particularly for technical and leadership roles across the portfolio.

### Key Responsibilities
* Manage the end-to-end recruitment process, from drafting job descriptions to offer negotiation and onboarding.
* Focus on strategic sourcing for hard-to-fill roles (e.g., CTOs, Senior Developers, experienced Founders).
* Partner with Founding Partners to define organizational structure and career paths for new ventures.
* Assist in maintaining company culture, employee engagement initiatives, and performance management cycles.

### Requirements
* 3+ years of experience in Talent Acquisition or a general HR role, preferably within a startup or technology environment.
* Strong interviewing and assessment skills.
* Proven ability to work autonomously and handle confidential information.
* Passionate about the DCI mission (Sharing, Green, Circular Economies).

### Our Commitment
Be the backbone of DCI's growth by finding the right leaders and builders who will scale our ventures to market leadership.
