### Overview
CarX is a project to develop online car sales through AI technology to make car shopping easier as well as experience car rental before buying (Rent2Buy, Buy after use) and cooperating to exploit cars after buying on the O2O automobile e-commerce platform.
### Vision
Become the pioneer and largest online car sales platform in Vietnam 
### Mission
Make car buying simpler, friendlier and more economical: 
- More people can access cars.  
- Optimize vehicle usage time
### Signature product 
Rent2Buy, Buy after use
### Customer segment 
B2C
