### Overview
NextU is a new paradigm of living, working, learning, and giving where art, business, and personal growth merge into one integrated, holistic human experience through a network of soulful co-living spaces.
It is a membership-based transformational living platform where people come together to live with purpose, create with passion, and connect with depth.
### Vision
To ignite a global movement of conscious, creative, and collective living through a vibrant network of co-living spaces.
### Mision
To empower the conscious, creative, and collective living spirit within the NEXT dreamers, creators, entrepreneurs, and change makers within a thriving global community.
