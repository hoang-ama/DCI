# Senior Full Stack Developer

### Company & Location
- Company: DC Tech (SaaS for Mobility & Logistics)
- Department: Technology
- Location: Hanoi, Vietnam (Remote flexibility available)
- Employment Type: Full-time

### Role Summary
Lead the development lifecycle for our core SaaS platform. You will be responsible for designing and implementing scalable, high-performance web applications that serve the entire DCI mobility ecosystem.

### Key Responsibilities
* Design and implement robust RESTful APIs using Node.js/Express.
* Develop user interfaces and dynamic front-end features using React.js.
* Manage and optimize relational and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL/MongoDB).
* Conduct thorough code reviews and mentor junior developers.
* Collaborate closely with product owners and designers to translate wireframes into functional features.

### Requirements
* 5+ years of experience in full-stack web development.
* Deep proficiency in Node.js, JavaScript/TypeScript, and React ecosystem.
* Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP) and CI/CD pipelines.
* Strong understanding of microservices architecture is a plus.

### Why Join DC Tech?
You will be building the core technology that powers Vietnam's leading shared mobility ventures, enjoying a high-impact role with clear growth opportunities.
