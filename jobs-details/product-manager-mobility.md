# Product Manager (Mobility)

### Company & Location
- Company: DiChung (Leading Shared Mobility Marketplace)
- Department: Business / Product
- Location: Hanoi, Vietnam
- Employment Type: Full-time

### Role Summary
You will own the product roadmap for a key segment of the DiChung platform (e.g., Carpooling or B2B Transfer). Your mission is to drive user adoption, optimize the booking funnel, and enhance matching algorithms to maximize sharing efficiency and reduce environmental impact.

### Key Responsibilities
* Define, prioritize, and execute the product roadmap based on market analysis and business goals.
* Conduct A/B testing and analyze key metrics (conversion, utilization, LTV) to inform decisions.
* Work with engineering and design teams to deliver high-quality, scalable product features.
* Gather user feedback and competitive intelligence to identify new growth opportunities.

### Requirements
* 3+ years of experience as a Product Manager in tech, preferably within the mobility, logistics, or marketplace sector.
* Proven ability to define and measure product success using data (SQL, Google Analytics/Amplitude).
* Excellent communication skills and ability to manage cross-functional stakeholders.

### Our Impact
Help millions of people travel more affordably and sustainably by optimizing vehicle usage across Vietnam.
